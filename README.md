# WBM · WRATH OF THE BLACK MANTA

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **HOBBY**.

Wrath of the Black Manta (developed by A.I Co., published by Taito, NES 1990) — the Western rework of the Famicom's 忍者COPサイゾウ Ninja Cop Saizou (1989). A Shinobi-style ninja game-world: a nameless ninja (Saizou at home) infiltrates the syndicate DRAT — Drug Runners And Terrorists — which abducts children, earning a new ninjutsu ART at each boss and INTERROGATING the goons he catches, until at the top the mastermind EL TORO ('the Bull') holds the child Taro as a human shield. THE FIRST OF THE NEW STANDARD: source-material theming (night/crimson NES-ninja palette, 8-bit/CRT scanlines, Press Start 2P), a BANNER marquee, and an SVG TITLE CARD (the black-manta silhouette + moon + the title drawn). Render-not-invent flags: A.I Co. developed (Taito only published); the JP story kidnaps children for WAR, the US version for DRUGS (a localization rewrite); the interrogation mechanic is genuine but famously NEAR-USELESS; Taro is a kidnapped CHILD, not the hero (common mix-up); the bosses beyond El Toro (Tiny, the Voodoo Warrior) are fan/guide names, flagged. 8 emergents across all four natures — the Black Manta · El Toro · DRAT · Taro · the Ninja Arts · the Interrogation · Tiny · the Voodoo Warrior.
